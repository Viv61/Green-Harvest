About Our Farm

Welcome to Green Harvest

Welcome to Green Harvest, a premier organic farm located in the serene landscapes of Kanakapura Taluk, Karnataka. Our commitment to sustainable agriculture and eco-friendly practices sets us apart in the organic farming community.

We take pride in cultivating premium-quality organic fruits, vegetables, and specialty crops through sustainable and eco-friendly agricultural practices that respect both nature and consumer health.

Our Expertise

At Green Harvest, we specialize in cultivating high-value plantation crops that include:

  • Agarwood (Aquilaria species) - Known for its precious aromatic properties
  • Dendrocalamus asper - Premium quality bamboo variety
  • Bambusa balcooa - Versatile bamboo species
  • Bambusa tulda - Traditional bamboo variety

Through these specialized cultivations, we produce superior edible bamboo shoots, construction-grade timber, sustainable biomass, and high-quality raw materials for the pulp industry.

Our Farming Approach

Our farming philosophy centers around three fundamental principles:

  • Sustainability: Implementing practices that preserve and enhance our natural resources
  • Biodiversity: Maintaining a diverse ecosystem that supports natural balance
  • Soil Health: Nurturing the soil through organic methods and crop rotation

We maintain a chemical-free, regenerative ecosystem through carefully planned organic techniques, innovative water conservation strategies, and responsible agroforestry practices.

Fresh from Our Fields

Our diverse range of organic products includes:

  • Seasonal Fruits: Fresh mangoes, guavas, papayas, bananas, pomegranates, and various citrus varieties
  • Nutritious Vegetables: Garden-fresh tomatoes, brinjals, gourds, leafy greens, beans, and root vegetables
  • Exotic and Medicinal Crops: Specially cultivated varieties with significant nutritional and medicinal benefits

Our Commitments

  • Sustainable Agriculture: We utilize organic compost, implement crop rotation, and employ natural pest management to preserve soil vitality
  • Chemical-Free Production: Our strict no-synthetic policy ensures pure, natural produce free from pesticides, herbicides, and artificial fertilizers
  • Eco-Conscious Practices: We integrate rainwater harvesting, agroforestry, and biodiversity conservation into our daily operations

Farm-to-Table Quality

We believe that the freshest food provides the most nutritional benefits. That's why we carefully harvest our produce at peak ripeness and deliver it directly to our customers.

Every product that leaves our farm is guaranteed to be at its optimal freshness, ensuring maximum flavor, nutrition, and quality for your table.
